# Elder Scrolls Online

Elder Scrolls Online, developed by ZeniMax Online Studios and published by Bethesda Softworks, is a massively multiplayer online game with over 20 million players. The official website serves as a central hub for this large gaming community, offering news, game guides, and forums.
 My role was to ensure a user-centric design for the website, facilitating easy navigation and engagement with the content for millions of monthly visitors.
