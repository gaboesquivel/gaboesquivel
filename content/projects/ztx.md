# ZTX

ZTX is a web3 metaverse platform backed by Jump Crypto and ZEPETO (a subsidiary of Naver Z Corp with over 200M users). The platform enables creators to build, play, and earn through blockchain-based NFT trading.
 As a software consultant in the early stages of ZTX, I was responsible for developing the react app to host the metaverse and the WebGL/EVM wallet integration.
